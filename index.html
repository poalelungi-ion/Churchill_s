Churchill’s Whisky Bar

Welcome to Churchill’s Whisky Bar, Chisinau’s ultimate destination for English cuisine, fine spirits, and live entertainment.

  • OPEN from 12:00 every day.
  • Indulge in authentic English dishes crafted to perfection.
  • Enjoy live music and vibrant evenings.
  • Taste our exclusive collection of whiskies and signature cocktails.

Located in the heart of Chisinau, Churchill’s Whisky Bar is a hidden gem for anyone looking to unwind in a stylish, intimate atmosphere. With an extensive collection of the finest whiskies, including rare labels and local favorites, our bar has earned a reputation as one of the best drink spots in town. Whether you're looking to try something new or stick with a classic cocktail, our experienced bartenders are here to make every drink an experience.

We’re more than just a bar – we’re a lounge where friends gather, conversations flow, and every visit feels like a special occasion. Stop by Mitropolit Gavril Bănulescu Bodoni 22 to experience the best of English hospitality, right here in Chisinau. Don’t miss out on our live music nights, where local and international artists set the perfect vibe for an unforgettable evening.
